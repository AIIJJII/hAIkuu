About Our App

You've just snapped the most artistic, stunning photo yet.
After applying Valencia on Instagram, you realize you have one problem: You don't have an artistic caption to go with it. Hipsters fear no more!!! hAIkuu sees and interprets your image, and uses in-house AI natural language processing algorithms to craft deep haikus that your artsy friends can't possibly criticize.

Creativity Engine

We use an autonomous engine that undergoes the creative process: inspiration and execution. Using Clarifai's image classifier and our in-house NLP haiku generator, we provide endless amounts of new poetry and fun.

Facebook Share

Never have caption-block or captionless photos again! Use hAIkuu to share photos and watch all your friends marvel at your poetic skills, even if it's an AI's haiku.

Twitter Integration

Is Facebook too mainstream? You could also tweet out your haikus and the pictures. You could even tweet the hashtag #haikuu and @haikuuapp will reply to you!
